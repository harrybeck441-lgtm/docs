Get from zero to your first AI call, contact, or workflow in just a few minutes.

## Create your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Head to SalesAI and create your account using any of these methods:

    - Email and password
    - Google account
    - Microsoft account
    - Team invite link (if your teammate has already shared one)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Secure your account">
    Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) with OTP verification for extra security. You can also use passwordless magic login links instead of a password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in and explore">
    Once logged in, you'll land on your dashboard with the built-in CRM, AI Voice Agents, Workflows, and Analytics ready to go.
  </Step>
</Steps>
