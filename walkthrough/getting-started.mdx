## Creating your account

- Sign up with email and password, a Google account, or a Microsoft account
- Join a team instantly by accepting an invite link
- Skip passwords entirely with magic login links sent to your inbox
- Keep things secure with two-factor authentication (2FA) and OTP verification
- Reset access easily through the forgot-password and reset flows
