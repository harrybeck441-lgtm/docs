## What you'll see

For agencies: how to white-label the entire SalesAI platform under your own brand, set up a custom domain, and manage multiple clients from a single dashboard — so you can resell AI-powered sales tools as your own product.

## Three steps to go white-label

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Create your agency account on SalesAI.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Brand it">
    Upload your logo, set your colours, and configure your domain.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resell">
    Onboard clients under your brand and start earning recurring revenue.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Branding panel

- Upload your agency logo (shown in the header and on emails)
- Set a custom favicon for browser tabs
- Add a separate email logo for outbound communications
- Choose a primary brand colour, applied across the whole UI
- Set your agency name for the login page and throughout the platform

## Custom domain

- Use your own domain (e.g. `app.youragency.com`) instead of the default URL
- Follow step-by-step DNS verification with copy-to-clipboard records
- Manage SSL certificates — request, verify, and track status
- Watch verification status update in real time (Pending / Verified)

## Multi-client dashboard

- See every client sub-account in one place
- Check each client's status — Active or Trial
- Track contact count and activity per client
- Monitor combined Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) across all accounts

## Billing, teams & branded login

- Agency-specific plans with higher limits for managing many clients
- Handle billing and payments from the admin panel
- Gate feature access with clear upgrade prompts
- Control which team members can see or manage specific clients
- Give clients a fully branded login page — with zero SalesAI branding visible
